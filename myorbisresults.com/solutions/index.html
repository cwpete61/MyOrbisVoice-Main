The MyOrbisResults suite is one system — MyOrbisVoice answers and books, MyOrbisReviews builds the reputation that earns the next call, MyOrbisLocal makes you show up when people search, and MyOrbisWeb + MyOrbisSocial turn that attention into booked work. Flagship · Live Today

MyOrbisVoice — your AI voice team that never misses a call.

Not a chatbot — a multi-agent voice team that answers, qualifies, and books across three channels: a website voice widget on your site, an inbound phone receptionist for the calls you can't take, and an outbound caller for follow-up and campaigns. It speaks naturally on Gemini Live, books straight into your Google Calendar, sends email and SMS confirmations, and logs every conversation into a built-in CRM.

Behind the voice sits a roster of specialized agents — Orchestrator, Appointment, Sales, Customer Service, Marketing, and Secretary — that hand off to each other like a real front desk. The whole team is driven by your Business DNA: a structured profile of your services, pricing, rules, and escalation conditions, so the agent answers like someone who actually works for you. It runs tag-driven, multi-channel campaigns across phone, SMS, and email — and it's bilingual English and Spanish end to end.
