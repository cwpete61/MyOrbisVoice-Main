Same Six Leaks, Different Wording

If a missed call is a missed job, this is built for you.

Every local service business runs on the same two engines: local search and the inbound call. A buyer opens Google or Maps, types "[service] near me," and calls one of the top three results in the Google Map Pack. The business that ranks, answers first, and follows up wins the job — the rest leak it. That pattern holds whether you fill chairs, book consults, dispatch trucks, or sell memberships. Below, the six leaks in each industry's own language, and the part of the system that plugs each one.

Dental

New-patient calls come during the workday — and go to voicemail during cleanings. The agent answers, books the chair, and confirms so the slot doesn't no-show. Reviews lift you above the practice down the street.

Fixes: Cold Leads · Invisible Online

Legal

An intake call is worth thousands, and the first firm to answer usually wins it. The agent captures the matter, screens for fit, and books the consult — day, night, or weekend — instead of letting it ring out.

Fixes: Cold Leads · No Sales Process

HVAC & Plumbing

Emergencies don't wait for business hours. The agent picks up at 2am, confirms availability, books the truck, and texts the confirmation — so the job goes to you, not the next number they call.

Fixes: Cold Leads · Dead Money

Roofing & Electrical

High-ticket jobs, long sales cycles, expensive leads. The system nurtures every quote request, follows up until they decide, and keeps your map ranking strong where storm-season searches spike.

Fixes: Wasted Marketing · Invisible Online

Fitness & Studios

Interest is impulsive — catch it now or lose it. The agent books the trial, reactivates lapsed members, and runs the follow-up sequence that turns a free class into a membership.

Fixes: Dead Money · Cold Leads

Med Spas, Salons & Beauty

Bookings, rebooking, and reviews drive everything. The agent fills the calendar, prompts the next appointment, and asks for the review at the right moment — while you focus on clients in the chair.

Fixes: Owner Drowning · Invisible Online

Home Services

You're on a roof or under a sink, not by the phone. The system answers, qualifies, books, and follows up so leads don't pile up unanswered while you're doing the work that pays.

Fixes: Owner Drowning · Wasted Marketing

Multi-Location Brands

Every location leaks differently and reports inconsistently. One system standardizes the customer journey, routes by location, and rolls results up so you can see where the revenue actually leaks.

Fixes: No Sales Process · the whole bucket
