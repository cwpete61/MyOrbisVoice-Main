## SECTION 1 — WHERE THE MONEY IS GOING

**Eyebrow:** The math, broken down

**Headline:** Here's where the money you just lost actually went.

That number above isn't theoretical. It's the result of four very specific leaks, all happening at the same time, every day, in your business:

**1. Calls you don't pick up.**
62% of business calls don't get answered. Lunch breaks. Weekends. After 5 PM. Your team's on another line. The caller hangs up. They don't leave a voicemail. They Google the next business in your category.

**2. After-hours callers you'll never hear from again.**
85% of people who call after hours and hit voicemail never call back. They book the next business on the list. By the time you check voicemail at 9 AM, that revenue is gone.

**3. The contacts you're not following up with.**
Your CRM has roughly four months of contacts you haven't reached out to. Each one was worth $200 to $500 to your business at the time. Most are now buying from somebody else.

**4. The customers you're not bringing back.**
A patient who skipped their six-month cleaning is worth thousands over the years they don't come back. A salon client who didn't rebook is worth $2,000+ over the year you don't see her. Most service businesses lose 20-30% of their customer base annually to silence.

Add those four leaks together and you get the number on the calculator above.
