Are prices in USD?

Yes. All prices are in US dollars ($). Monthly plans are billed monthly.

What's included in the Free plan?

The Free plan lets you try the website widget with one voice agent and limited monthly usage, so you can set up your Business DNA and hear the system answer before you commit.

Which plan should I start with?

Most businesses start on Pro — it turns on all three channels (widget, inbound, and outbound) plus CRM and campaigns. If the phone is your whole front desk, Basic covers inbound; if you run multiple locations, Premier or Enterprise scale with you.

Can I change plans later?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade as your volume changes — higher tiers include everything below them, so moving up just adds more minutes, agents, channels, and campaigns.

How are the other products priced?

MyOrbisReviews, MyOrbisLocal, MyOrbisWeb, and MyOrbisSocial are scoped to your business in a free consultation. Pricing depends on your locations, volume, and goals — book a call and we'll give you a clear number.
