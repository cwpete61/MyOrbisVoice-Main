Are prices in USD?

Yes. All prices are in US dollars ($), shown as a per-month figure. You can pay monthly or yearly.

How does monthly vs. yearly billing work?

Use the Monthly / Yearly toggle at the top to switch the prices. Yearly billing saves 16.7% — you pay 10 months' worth for a full year, billed once annually.

What's included in the Free plan?

The Free plan lets you try the website widget with one voice agent and limited monthly usage, so you can set up your Business DNA and hear the system answer before you commit.

Which plan should I start with?

Most businesses start on Pro — it turns on all three channels (widget, inbound, and outbound) plus CRM and campaigns. If the phone is your whole front desk, Basic covers inbound; if you run multiple locations or need custom volume, Premier scales with you and Enterprise is sized by appointment.

Can I change plans later?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade as your volume changes — higher tiers include everything below them, so moving up just adds more minutes, agents, channels, and campaigns.

How are MyOrbisReviews, MyOrbisLocal, and MyOrbisWeb priced?

They have published monthly prices — MyOrbisReviews $297, MyOrbisLocal $497, and MyOrbisWeb $997 — and the same monthly-or-yearly billing as Voice. Bundle all of them with Voice in the MyOrbisTrifecta for $4,997/mo, fully managed.

What about Enterprise?

Enterprise is sized to your business — custom call and campaign volume, multi-location routing, SLAs, and dedicated support. There's no fixed price; book an appointment and we'll scope it with you.

One consultation, and we'll size the system to your business — and show you exactly what it costs to plug the leaks.
