MyOrbisVoice ("we," "us," or "our") operates the MyOrbisVoice platform, including the website at myorbisvoice.com and the application at app.myorbisvoice.com (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

By accessing or using our Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Service.

1. Information We Collect

Account and Registration Data

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, password (hashed and never stored in plain text), and business name. If you subscribe to a paid plan, payment details are processed directly by Stripe — we do not store raw credit card numbers.

Business Configuration Data

To operate your voice agents, we store configuration data you provide, including your Business DNA (business description, service catalog, operating hours), prompt content, agent settings, and channel configurations (website widget, inbound phone, outbound campaigns).

Call and Session Data

When your voice agents handle calls or widget sessions, we collect call transcripts, session summaries, call duration, outcome data, and any information captured during the interaction (such as appointment details or contact information provided by callers).

Contact Data

You may import or create contact records within the platform. This data is stored on your behalf and associated with your tenant account.

Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use the Service, including IP address (hashed for analytics), browser type, operating system, pages visited, features used, and timestamps of activity.

Affiliate Data

If you participate in our affiliate program, we collect payout method information, referral activity (click counts, conversions), and commission records. Payout details are stored securely and used only for processing your earnings.

Integration Credentials

When you connect third-party services (Google Calendar, Google Gmail, Twilio), we store OAuth tokens or API credentials in encrypted form. We never display these credentials in plaintext within the user interface.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate the Service — powering your voice agents, routing calls, processing bookings, and delivering core platform features.
  • Billing and Payments — processing subscriptions, issuing invoices, and managing plan entitlements via Stripe.
  • Customer Support — diagnosing issues, responding to inquiries, and improving your experience.
  • Service Improvement — analyzing aggregate usage patterns to improve features and reliability. We do not sell or use individual conversation content to train AI models without your explicit consent.
  • Communications — sending transactional emails (account confirmations, billing receipts, appointment reminders) via a dedicated sending subdomain isolated from staff mailboxes.
  • Legal Compliance — meeting our obligations under applicable law, including responding to lawful requests from authorities.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers — infrastructure partners (hosting, database, email delivery, AI inference) who process data on our behalf under data processing agreements.
  • Twilio — phone call audio and metadata is transmitted through Twilio's communications infrastructure. Twilio's own privacy policy governs their handling of that data.
  • Google — if you connect a Google account, calendar and email operations are performed via Google's APIs under your authorization.
  • Stripe — payment information is handled directly by Stripe. We receive only the outcome of payment events.
  • Legal Obligations — we may disclose your data if required by law, subpoena, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of MyOrbisVoice, our users, or the public.
  • Business Transfers — in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user data may be transferred as part of that transaction. You will be notified via email and a notice on our website.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account remains active. If you close your account, we will delete or anonymize your data within 90 days, except where we are required by law to retain it longer (for example, billing records required for tax purposes).

Call transcripts and session data are retained for 12 months by default. You may request earlier deletion by contacting us.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption in transit, AES-256 encryption at rest for sensitive credentials, hashed passwords using bcrypt, short-lived session tokens, and role-based access controls. We log all admin impersonation actions and integration changes.

No system is perfectly secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at admin@myorbisvoice.com.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Authentication — maintaining your login session.
  • Preferences — remembering your settings.
  • Affiliate Tracking — a first-party cookie with a configurable duration (default 30 days) to attribute referrals from our affiliate partners. No cross-site tracking.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or behavioral tracking pixels. For more detail see our Cookie Policy.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to certain processing activities.
  • Withdraw Consent — where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at admin@myorbisvoice.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we learn we have inadvertently collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or a prominent notice on the Service at least 14 days before taking effect. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
