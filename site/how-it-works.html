How It Works

From signup to live calls in under an hour

No developers. No complex integrations. Just answer a few questions and your AI voice agent is ready to go.

Setup

Get your agent configured

1

Create your account

Sign up at app.myorbisvoice.com. Choose your plan and your workspace is ready in seconds. No credit card required to start.

2

Set your Business DNA

Tell your agent about your business — your services, pricing, hours, policies, and tone. This becomes the knowledge base for every conversation your agent has.

3

Configure your agents

Choose which agent roles you need — receptionist, appointment setter, sales, customer service. Each role has a focused purpose and can be customised to your workflow.

4

Connect your channels

Enable the website widget, link your Twilio phone number for inbound calls, or set up an outbound campaign. Each channel is activated independently — start with one and expand.

5

Connect Google Calendar

Authorise your Google account and your agent can check availability and book appointments in real time — during the call, without human involvement.

6

Go live

Embed the widget snippet on your site, point your phone number, or launch your first outbound campaign. Your agent is now answering, booking, and following up — around the clock.

FAQ

Common questions

Do I need a developer to set this up? +
No. The entire setup happens inside your dashboard. If you want to add the website widget, you paste one line of code — that's the only technical step.
Can the agent really book appointments on my calendar? +
Yes. Once you connect your Google Calendar via OAuth, your agent can check your real-time availability and book directly during the call. Confirmation emails go out automatically.
What if the caller asks something my agent doesn't know? +
The agent is trained on your Business DNA but also has escalation rules. If it can't answer, it can take a message, book a callback, or warm-transfer to you — based on your configured preferences.
How long does the voice agent actually sound natural? +
MyOrbisVoice uses Gemini Live for real-time voice, which produces natural, low-latency speech. Most callers won't know they're talking to an AI unless you tell them.
Can I use my existing phone number? +
You can forward your existing number to your Twilio number, or port your number to Twilio. Either way, calls to your number get answered by your AI agent.
Is my customer data private? +
Yes. Your workspace data is fully isolated — no other customer can see or access your conversations, contacts, or configuration. All data is encrypted at rest.
