Campaign automation

Tag a contact. Watch the right message fire.

Every call outcome auto-tags the contact. Every tag triggers a campaign. Every campaign fans out across phone, SMS, email, and WhatsApp — independently, on a schedule, with template substitution.

Four campaigns ship pre-built and email-ready on day one:

  • Booking Confirmation — fires instantly when a call ends with outcome BOOKED. "Hi {firstName}, your appointment with {businessName} is confirmed for {appointmentDate} at {appointmentTime}."
  • Day-Before Reminder — fires 24 hours before any appointment.startAt. Auto-enrolled by the booking system.
  • Callback Follow-Up — 4-hour delay after outcome CALLBACK_REQUESTED. Soft re-engagement.
  • Missed-Call Follow-Up — instant when outcome is MISSED_CALL. "Sorry we missed you — here's a number to reach us back."
