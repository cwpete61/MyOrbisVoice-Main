Business DNA

Generic AI sounds generic. MyOrbisVoice sounds like you.

Most "AI receptionist" tools paste your business name into a generic script. MyOrbisVoice uses a five-layer prompt architecture that braids platform safety rules, your business DNA, the channel context, the agent's specialized role, and live session data into every response.

  • Your services, pricing, hours, escalation rules — captured as structured data, versioned, published with one click.
  • Per-channel personality — the widget agent flirts a bit, the inbound receptionist is warm and brisk, the outbound campaign agent stays under 30 seconds.
  • Per-role specialization — appointment agents know your booking rules, sales agents know your pricing, customer-service agents know your refund policy.
  • Compliance language baked in — prohibited phrases, mandatory disclaimers, opt-out keywords all enforced at the prompt layer.

Campaign automation

Tag a contact. Watch the right message fire.

Every call outcome auto-tags the contact. Every tag triggers a campaign. Every campaign fans out across phone, SMS, email, and WhatsApp — independently, on a schedule, with template substitution.

Four campaigns ship pre-built and email-ready on day one:

  • Booking Confirmation — fires instantly when a call ends with outcome BOOKED. "Hi {firstName}, your appointment with {businessName} is confirmed for {appointmentDate} at {appointmentTime}."
  • Day-Before Reminder — fires 24 hours before any appointment.startAt. Auto-enrolled by the booking system.
  • Callback Follow-Up — 4-hour delay after outcome CALLBACK_REQUESTED. Soft re-engagement.
  • Missed-Call Follow-Up — instant when outcome is MISSED_CALL. "Sorry we missed you — here's a number to reach us back."
