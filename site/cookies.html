Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026  ·  Effective: April 30, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how MyOrbisVoice ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website at myorbisvoice.com or use our application at app.myorbisvoice.com.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website when you visit it. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide basic analytics to site operators. Cookies are not programs — they cannot execute code or transmit viruses.

Similar technologies include:

  • Local Storage / Session Storage — browser-based key/value storage used to persist login tokens and UI state.
  • Pixel tags / Web beacons — we do not currently use these.
  • Fingerprinting — we do not fingerprint devices.

2. Cookies We Use

We use only first-party cookies. We do not use third-party advertising or behavioral tracking cookies.

Cookie / Key Purpose Duration Type
va_access_token Stores your JWT access token to keep you logged in during an active session. 15 minutes (refreshed automatically) Strictly Necessary
va_refresh_token Stores your opaque refresh token so you can obtain new access tokens without re-entering your password. 30 days Strictly Necessary
ref Stores an affiliate referral code when you arrive via an affiliate link. Used to attribute new signups to the referring affiliate. Configurable by platform admin (default: 30 days) Functional / Affiliate Tracking
nav-mobile state (sessionStorage) Remembers whether the mobile nav menu is open during your current session. Not persisted after the tab closes. Session only Functional

3. Strictly Necessary Cookies

Some cookies are essential for the Service to function. These include the authentication tokens described above. Without them, you cannot log in or use the application. These cookies do not require your consent under most privacy frameworks and cannot be disabled without breaking core Service functionality.

4. Affiliate Tracking Cookies

When you click a referral link from an affiliate partner, we set a first-party ref cookie on your device. This cookie:

  • Contains only the affiliate's referral code — no personal data.
  • Is scoped to myorbisvoice.com and app.myorbisvoice.com only.
  • Is never shared with third parties.
  • Is used solely to credit the referring affiliate if you sign up during the cookie's lifetime.
  • Expires after the duration set by our platform admins (default 30 days). The current duration is shown on our Partner Program page.

This cookie does not track your browsing behavior across websites.

5. Analytics

We do not currently use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or any third-party analytics service that sets cookies. Any usage analytics we collect are aggregate and server-side, using hashed IP addresses.

6. Managing and Deleting Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View cookies currently set on your device.
  • Delete individual cookies or all cookies for a site.
  • Block cookies from specific sites or all third-party cookies.
  • Receive a notification before a cookie is placed.

Instructions for major browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Note: disabling strictly necessary cookies will prevent you from logging in to the Service.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our use of cookies changes or as required by law. We will post the updated policy here with a revised "Last updated" date. For material changes we will provide notice as described in our Privacy Policy.
