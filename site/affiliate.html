Program details

Everything you need to know

Commission rate +
30% of the monthly subscription fee for every active referral. Applies to Growth ($49/mo → $14.70 commission) and Enterprise ($149/mo → $44.70 commission).
Cookie duration +
60 days. If someone clicks your link and signs up any time within 60 days, that referral is credited to you.
Payout schedule +
Commissions are paid on the 15th of each month for the previous month's confirmed revenue. Minimum threshold is $50.
Payment methods +
PayPal and direct bank transfer (ACH/SEPA). You set your preferred payment method in your affiliate dashboard.
Is there a cap on earnings? +
No cap. There's no limit on how many referrals you can make or how much you can earn.
What if a referral upgrades their plan? +
Your commission adjusts automatically. If they upgrade from Growth to Enterprise, your monthly commission increases from $14.70 to $44.70.
