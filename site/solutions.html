Channel 3 · Outbound

Tag a contact. Watch the right message fire.

Every call outcome auto-tags the contact. Every tag triggers a campaign. Every campaign fans out across phone, SMS, email, and WhatsApp — independently, on a schedule, with template substitution baked in.

Four campaigns ship pre-built and email-ready on day one:

  • Booking Confirmation — fires instantly on outcome BOOKED. Your customer gets the confirmation before they've put the phone down.
  • Day-Before Reminder — auto-enrolled by the booking system. Fires 24 hours before appointment.startAt.
  • Callback Follow-Up — 4-hour delay after CALLBACK_REQUESTED. Soft re-engagement, not a hard sell.
  • Missed-Call Follow-Up — instant on outcome MISSED_CALL. Reaches them before they call your competitor.

Build unlimited custom campaigns. Use template tokens like {firstName}, {businessName}, {appointmentDate}, {appointmentTime} — substitute live per send.

Specialized agents

Seven role-specialized agents. Each one knows its job.

MyOrbisVoice doesn't run one generic agent — it runs the right agent for the job. Each role plugs into any channel and ships with sensible defaults.

Orchestrator

Routes complex calls. Decides which specialist handles which question. The traffic controller for everything else.

Appointment

Knows your hours, your booking rules, your buffer times. Books on Google Calendar with conflict checking, sends confirmation email + Calendar invite.

Sales

Quotes prices, qualifies budget and timeline, books discovery calls. Hands off cleanly to a human closer when ready to sign.

Customer Service

Resolves common issues, processes refunds within policy, escalates outliers. Reads from your written rules — not a stale FAQ doc.

Marketing

Runs outbound campaigns: re-engagement, recall, post-purchase check-ins. Drops the call gracefully if interest is low.

Assistant

Answers general questions, takes messages, transfers calls to the right human, handles after-hours overflow without overpromising.

Secretary

The default front-desk role. Greets, qualifies, books, takes messages. The agent most small businesses live on day one.

Plugs into your stack

Connects to the tools you already use.

MyOrbisVoice rides on top of your existing phone, calendar, and email — it doesn't replace them. Setup is OAuth-grade, not webhook-spaghetti.

Google Calendar

Live freebusy lookup, real event creation, full conflict checking. OAuth — no API keys to copy-paste.

Gmail

Confirmation emails, reminders, follow-ups all sent from your connected business address. OAuth scoped, no plaintext passwords.

Per-tenant subaccounts. Inbound, outbound, SMS, recording. Every call is isolated to your number, your trust score, your billing.

Stripe

Self-serve subscription billing. Plan switching, prorated upgrades, cancel anytime — handled by the standard Stripe Customer Portal.

Gemini 2.5 Flash

Native-audio model under the hood — sub-second response latency. We absorb the AI compute cost; you don't pay per token.

Webhooks & REST API

Pro plan and up. Pipe call events, transcripts, and outcomes into your CRM, helpdesk, or whatever else lives on the other side.
