Common questions

Pricing FAQ.

Straight answers about how billing actually works.

What counts as a "voice minute"?
A voice minute is one minute of an active call — inbound, outbound, or widget — between your AI agent and a person. Time on hold or in voicemail doesn't count. We bill in 6-second increments (so a 1:18 call costs 1.3 minutes, not 2). Minutes are pooled across all your phone numbers and reset on the 1st of each month.
What happens if I go over my monthly minutes?
We don't shut you off mid-call. Overages bill at $0.05/min on Basic, $0.04/min on Pro, and $0.03/min on Premier and above — added to next month's invoice. You'll get an in-app warning at 80% of your limit and an email at 95%, so you can upgrade if your volume is consistently higher than your plan.
Is the LTD (Lifetime Deal) really lifetime?
Yes — one $497 payment, every Premier-tier feature unlocked for the life of the platform. Limited to the first 100 customers, then it's gone. The LTD covers the same entitlement set as Premier ($997/mo) including sentiment analysis, VIP caller recognition, priority support, 5,000 monthly voice minutes, and 20,000 contacts. You're locked in at the LTD limits — no future tier auto-upgrade — but you also never get a price increase.
Do I need a phone number, or can I use my existing one?
Both work. You can buy a fresh number from us in 30 seconds (any US area code, included in your plan), or forward calls from your existing business line to your MyOrbisVoice number. Most customers do the second so they never have to update letterhead, Google Business, or printed cards.
Is there a free trial?
Yes — every account starts on the Free tier with 20 voice minutes and the website widget. No credit card required. You can build out your Business DNA, configure your agents, test the flow on the widget, and only enter payment details when you upgrade to add a real phone number.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your billing page in two clicks. No phone calls, no retention specialists. You keep access until the end of the current billing period; nothing pro-rates back. We never auto-renew without notice — every renewal sends a receipt 24 hours ahead.
What's the difference between LTD and Premier?
They unlock the exact same feature set and entitlements. The difference is billing: LTD is one $497 payment, Premier is $997/month. LTD will never get a price increase but also never auto-upgrades to higher tiers. Premier evolves as we ship new features and is the right pick if you want continuous expansion.
Do you charge for SMS or WhatsApp messages separately?
A2P 10DLC (US business SMS) carrier fees pass through at cost — currently about $0.0079 per outbound SMS. WhatsApp Business message fees pass through too (varies by destination country). On all plans where SMS/WhatsApp are enabled (Pro and above), the MyOrbisVoice platform fee is $0; you only pay the carrier rate.
Do voice minutes include AI compute costs?
Yes. The minute rate is all-in: Twilio's voice termination, our gateway routing, the Gemini 2.5 Flash native-audio inference, and the recording + transcript storage. Nothing à la carte. The only thing not included is Twilio's per-call connection fee in some unusual cases (premium-rate numbers, international); those rare cases pass through at cost with notice.
Can I switch plans?
Anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately and pro-rate the upgrade cost. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. We never charge a "plan change" fee.
Is there a non-profit or education discount?
Yes — 30% off any monthly plan for verified 501(c)(3) non-profits and accredited educational institutions. Email hello@myorbisvoice.com with proof of status to get a discount code.
