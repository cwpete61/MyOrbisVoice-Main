FAQ

Pricing questions

Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Cancel at any time from your billing settings. Your plan stays active until the end of the billing period — no penalties, no questions.
What counts as a conversation? +
A conversation is one continuous session — whether that's a phone call, a website widget session, or an outbound call attempt. Multiple exchanges within a single session count as one conversation.
Is there a free trial on paid plans? +
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 14 days of your first payment for a full refund.
Do I need to bring my own Twilio account? +
Yes. Twilio charges for phone calls and SMS separately from your MyOrbisVoice subscription. You connect your Twilio account and your call costs flow through them directly — we don't mark them up.
Can I upgrade or downgrade mid-cycle? +
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
