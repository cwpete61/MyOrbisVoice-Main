# MyOrbisResults Explainer — Presenter Script

Voice: Orby (Gemini "Aoede"), presenter PiP. 18 scenes, in order. One paragraph per scene. Matches `Mor-Explainer` / `Mor-Explainer-9x16` (narration files `public/narration/NN.wav`).

---

**01 · Hook**
Most local businesses don't have one problem. They're leaking revenue in six different places at once — and they can't see most of them. I'm Orby. Let me show you where it's going, and the one system that gets it back.

**02 · The Problem (bucket)**
You don't have one problem. You have a bucket with six holes. Each one drains a little. Together, they bleed your business dry.

**03 · Leak 1 — Dead Money**
Leak one: dead money. Every lead you collected and never followed up with is money already spent that produced nothing. They didn't say no — they just never heard from you again.

**04 · Leak 2 — Invisible Online**
Leak two: invisible online. If you're not in the top three of the Google Map Pack for "service near me," buyers never see you. Thin reviews keep you off the page where the decision happens.

**05 · Leak 3 — Cold Leads**
Leak three: cold leads. A ready-to-buy caller hits voicemail, or waits hours for a callback. By then they've booked the competitor who answered. Speed to lead is who wins the job.

**06 · Leak 4 — No Sales Process**
Leak four: no sales process. Calls get winged. One person books everyone, another books no one, and nobody knows why. Without a repeatable process, close rates stay low.

**07 · Leak 5 — Wasted Marketing**
Leak five: wasted marketing. You pay for ads and referrals, the leads come in, and then nothing. No nurture, no follow-up. The spend converts a fraction of what it should.

**08 · Leak 6 — Owner Drowning**
Leak six: the owner, drowning. Answering phones, chasing quotes, posting reviews at eleven at night — instead of selling and serving. That busywork is the most expensive leak of all.

**09 · Why Patching Fails**
Here's the trap: fix one hole, and the bucket still drains. A chatbot doesn't fix invisibility. SEO doesn't answer the phone. Only a connected system seals all six at once.

**10 · The System**
That's what we built. One system. Every leak sealed. Three products — Local, Web, and Voice — that feed each other, plus staff training to make it stick, so your growth compounds.

**11 · MyOrbisLocal — Get Found**
One: MyOrbisLocal gets you found. We optimize your Google Business Profile, audit your competitors and reviews, and move you into the map pack — and keep you there.

**12 · MyOrbisWeb — Convert Demand**
Two: MyOrbisWeb converts the demand. A site that mirrors your Google profile, with clear booking and call actions, that stays in sync as your profile changes.

**13 · MyOrbisVoice — Answer & Book**
Three: MyOrbisVoice answers and books. That's me. I pick up in under a second — phone, website, or booking page — qualify the caller, book on your real calendar, and follow up by email and text, in English or Spanish. This part is live and deep today.

**14 · Staff Training — Make It Stick**
Four: staff training makes it stick. We train your team on the process, so the system runs inside your business, not just around it.

**15 · The Trifecta (three working together)**
Here's how they work together. MyOrbisLocal gets you found, and feeds what it learns to MyOrbisVoice. Voice answers, books, and turns every conversation into data. That data sharpens your website and your local content. Three products, one loop — and a competitor with just one piece can't catch it.

**16 · The Funnel**
From the first search to repeat revenue, every stage hands off to the next — so the funnel keeps refilling itself, instead of drying up the moment you stop hustling.

**17 · Industries**
And it works for any business the phone actually runs — dental, legal, home services, fitness, beauty, and more. The same six leaks, just different wording.

**18 · CTA**
So stop patching, and start compounding. Book a free Trifecta Consultation at myorbisresults.com. I'll take it from there.
