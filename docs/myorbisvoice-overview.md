# MyOrbisVoice — Service Overview

## What Is MyOrbisVoice?

MyOrbisVoice is an AI-powered voice automation platform built for businesses that can't afford to miss a call, lose a lead, or leave a customer waiting.

It gives every business — regardless of size — a professional, always-on AI voice agent that answers calls, books appointments, follows up with leads, and handles routine customer conversations in real time. No hold music. No voicemail. No missed opportunity.

The platform runs across three channels:

- **Website Widget** — a voice agent embedded directly on your website, so visitors can speak to your business instantly without picking up a phone
- **Inbound Receptionist** — an AI that answers every incoming call, handles questions, routes callers, and books appointments 24 hours a day
- **Outbound Caller** — an AI that reaches out to your leads and customers with personalized calls for follow-ups, reminders, and campaigns

## Why Every Business Needs This

### The Problem Every Business Faces

**42% of business calls go unanswered.**

When a potential customer calls and reaches voicemail — or worse, rings endlessly — the majority don't leave a message. They call your competitor instead. That's not a customer service problem. That's a revenue problem.

Meanwhile, the staff who do answer phones spend hours every week on calls that follow predictable patterns: booking appointments, answering the same five questions, confirming hours, taking down information. That time has a cost.

And after hours? The business is dark. Leads that come in at 7pm wait until morning — if they wait at all.

### What MyOrbisVoice Changes

**Every call is answered.** The AI agent picks up immediately, speaks naturally, and handles the conversation from start to finish — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, on any volume of simultaneous calls.

**Every lead gets a follow-up.** The outbound caller reaches out to new leads within minutes of inquiry, before interest fades and before competitors make contact.

**Appointments get booked automatically.** The agent connects to Google Calendar, checks availability in real time, and books confirmed appointments without human involvement.

**Staff focus on high-value work.** Routine calls are handled by the AI. Staff handle exceptions, complex situations, and relationship-building — the work that actually requires a human.

**Nothing falls through the cracks.** Every call is logged. Every conversation is transcribed. Every appointment is recorded. Owners and managers have full visibility.

## What Makes MyOrbisVoice Different

**It sounds like a real person.**
Built on Google's Gemini Live audio model, the agent speaks naturally with real-time audio — not pre-recorded prompts, not robotic text-to-speech. Callers routinely don't realize they're speaking with AI.

**It knows your business.**
Each agent is configured with your Business DNA — your services, pricing, rules, hours, escalation conditions, and language preferences. It speaks specifically about your business, not generic responses.

**It works across every channel.**
One platform covers your website, your phone line, and your outbound campaigns. Configuration is centralized. Results are unified.

**Every tenant is isolated.**
Each business runs in its own workspace with its own configuration, its own phone numbers, its own integrations, and its own data. Nothing is shared between customers.

**It integrates with the tools you already use.**
Google Calendar for appointments. Gmail for confirmations. Twilio for calls and SMS. Stripe for billing. No ripping out existing systems.

**It scales without adding headcount.**
Whether you handle 10 calls a week or 10,000, the cost structure stays flat. There's no hiring, no training, no sick days, no turnover.
