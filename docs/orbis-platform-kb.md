## What MyOrbisVoice is

MyOrbisVoice is an AI voice automation platform for service businesses.
It runs the customer-facing operations of a small-to-mid service business:
an AI voice receptionist that answers the phone, an outbound caller that
calls customers back, a website voice widget, calendar booking, follow-up
campaigns by SMS and email, a built-in CRM, and full customer history.

In plain terms: it replaces or compresses several separate tools a business
used to juggle — a receptionist, a scheduling assistant, an SMS marketing
tool, an email follow-up tool, a basic CRM, and a missed-call recovery
service — into one platform driven by one AI brain.

The voice engine is called **Orby**, MyOrbisVoice's proprietary real-time
voice engine.

Typical customers: dental offices, law firms, home-services companies,
fitness studios, and beauty businesses — places where a missed call is
lost revenue and where Spanish-speaking customers are a meaningful share
of the calls.

MyOrbisVoice is a product of MyOrbisResults.

## The three voice channels

1. **Inbound voice** — phone calls answered by the AI receptionist.
2. **Outbound voice** — the agent calls customers for the business:
   appointment confirmations, callback follow-ups, missed-call recovery,
   lead nurture.
3. **Website widget** — visitors click a microphone button on the
   business's own website and have the same voice conversation, no phone
   call required. Also installable as a one-click WordPress plugin.

There is also a **public booking page** for people who would rather type
than talk: a date picker and time-slot grid that respects the business's
working hours, plus a contact form. It is bilingual (English/Spanish).

Messaging channels — SMS and email — send follow-ups through the same AI
system. Email is fully live through Gmail. WhatsApp is coming soon.

## What makes it more than a chatbot

- **Seven specialized AI agent roles** per business — Orchestrator,
  Appointment, Sales, Customer Service, Marketing, Assistant, Secretary.
  The Orchestrator routes a live caller to the right specialist mid-call,
  invisibly.
- **Business DNA** — each business fills in structured fields (identity,
  services, pricing, operating rules, booking rules, compliance) that the
  agent follows consistently on every call and every channel.
- **Live integrations** — Google Calendar (real availability, real
  bookings), Gmail (real follow-up emails), Stripe (billing), Twilio
  (phone numbers and SMS).
- **Knowledge base** — a business can upload its own documents (PDF, Word,
  Excel, text) and the agent answers from them.
- **CRM + campaigns** — every call creates or updates a contact record
  with recording, transcript, AI summary, and outcome. Tags trigger
  automated multi-channel follow-up campaigns.
- **Bilingual end to end** — every screen, email, and help article exists
  in English and Spanish.

Every call ends with a recording, a full transcript, and an AI-written
summary, so the business can listen and verify what the agent did.

## Billing details callers commonly ask about

- **What is a voice minute?** One minute of an active call between the AI
  agent and a person. Time on hold or in voicemail does not count. Billed
  in 6-second increments. Minutes are pooled across all the business's
  phone numbers and reset on the 1st of each month.
- **Going over the minute limit:** calls are never cut off mid-call.
  Overage rates: $0.05/min on Basic, $0.04/min on Pro, $0.03/min on
  Premier and above, added to next month's invoice. An in-app warning
  fires at 80% of the limit and an email at 95%.
- **SMS and WhatsApp:** the MyOrbisVoice platform fee for messages is $0
  on plans where messaging is enabled (Pro and above). Only the carrier
  rate passes through at cost — currently about $0.0079 per outbound SMS.
- **Phone numbers:** a business can buy a fresh US number in about 30
  seconds (any US area code) or forward calls from its existing business
  line to its MyOrbisVoice number. Most customers forward, so they never
  have to change their printed materials or Google listing.
- **Switching plans:** anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately and
  pro-rate; downgrades take effect at the end of the billing period. No
  plan-change fee.
- **Cancelling:** two clicks from the billing page. Access continues to
  the end of the current billing period.
- **Non-profit / education discount:** 30% off any monthly plan for
  verified 501(c)(3) non-profits and accredited schools — email
  hello@myorbisvoice.com with proof of status.
