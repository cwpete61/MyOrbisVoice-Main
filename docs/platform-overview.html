Turn Every Call Into a Business Opportunity — Automatically

MyOrbisVoice is a full-stack, multi-tenant AI voice automation platform that answers your phones, handles your website visitors, and runs your outbound calling campaigns — around the clock, without hiring additional staff. Every interaction is logged, transcribed, summarized, and available instantly from your dashboard.

What Is MyOrbisVoice?

MyOrbisVoice replaces or augments your front-line communications team with a configurable AI voice agent. The agent knows your business — your services, pricing, hours, rules, and tone — and handles inbound calls, web visitors, and outbound follow-up campaigns exactly the way you would, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

It is not a call center. It is not a chatbot. It is a trained, real-time voice AI that speaks, listens, books appointments, answers questions, qualifies leads, and escalates to a human when necessary — all within a single platform your team manages from a browser.

Three Communication Channels

📞

Inbound Receptionist

Answers every inbound call, greets callers by your brand, handles questions, books appointments, and escalates to a live person when needed. Fully after-hours capable.

🌐

Website Voice Widget

📤

Outbound Caller

Runs automated outbound calling campaigns to your contact lists. Follow-ups, reminders, re-engagement, and lead qualification — executed at scale with no manual dialing.

Core Platform Features

Business DNA — Your Agent Knows Your Business

Every tenant configures a Business DNA profile — a structured knowledge base that covers your business identity, services, pricing, operating hours, lead qualification rules, appointment logic, escalation conditions, language preferences, compliance rules, and prohibited topics. The AI agent draws from this in every conversation without you needing to script every possible interaction.

AI Voice Agent — Real-Time, Human-Quality Conversations

Powered by Google Gemini Live for real-time voice understanding and generation, and OpenAI GPT-4o for reasoning and orchestration. The agent listens, responds, handles interruptions naturally, detects silence, and knows when to transfer, book, or escalate. Conversations feel natural — not robotic.

Appointment Booking — Fully Automated

The agent books appointments directly into Google Calendar during the call. It checks live availability, offers alternative slots, confirms the booking verbally, and sends a confirmation. No manual follow-up required.

Call Transcripts & Summaries

Every conversation — inbound, outbound, and widget — is automatically:

  • Transcribed word-for-word, line by line, labeled Caller and Agent
  • Summarized in 2–3 sentences by AI for quick review
  • Stored and searchable from the Conversations dashboard
  • Available for playback via voice recording

Voice Recordings

All calls are recorded and stored securely in cloud storage (Bunny.net). Recordings are accessible from the conversation history with a signed playback link. Storage quotas are managed per account with configurable retention policies.

Contact Management

A full contact database with call history, SMS history, opt-out tracking per channel (voice, SMS, email), email validation, and a complete activity timeline per contact. Contacts are automatically matched to inbound callers.

SMS — Two-Way Messaging

Send and receive SMS messages through the platform. Inbound STOP/START keywords are automatically detected and processed. Opt-out status is enforced before any outbound SMS is sent. All messages are logged to the contact timeline.

Outbound Campaigns

Build and launch outbound call campaigns to your contact list. Configure the calling script, target audience, retry policy, and scheduling. Campaign results, outcomes, and recordings are tracked per call.

AI Agent Roles

Each business configures one or more agent roles, each with its own personality, prompt stack, and allowed actions:

  • Orchestrator — routes and coordinates between other agents
  • Appointment Agent — focused on booking, rescheduling, and calendar management
  • Sales Agent — lead qualification, product presentation, objection handling
  • Customer Service Agent — issue resolution, account questions, support escalation
  • Marketing Agent — campaign outreach, promotions, survey collection
  • Assistant — general-purpose help for any caller need
  • Secretary — message taking, transferring, scheduling

Integrations

Google (Gmail & Calendar)

Connect a dedicated Google account via OAuth. The AI agent sends booking confirmations, follow-up emails, and manages calendar events directly — no manual intervention required. Credentials are stored encrypted and never exposed.

Twilio

All phone calls and SMS messages are handled through Twilio. Inbound call routing, outbound dialing, SMS delivery, call recording, voicemail, and call forwarding are all managed natively through the platform with no additional configuration required.

Stripe

Subscription billing, plan upgrades, and payment management are handled through Stripe. Tenants can manage their own billing from the dashboard including upgrading, downgrading, and accessing invoices through the Stripe customer portal.

OpenAI & Google Gemini

OpenAI GPT-4o powers agent reasoning, call summarization, and orchestration. Google Gemini Live powers real-time voice — the actual spoken conversation. Both are configured at the platform level and available to every tenant automatically.

Platform Administration

A full admin control surface is available to platform operators — separate from the tenant-facing dashboard. Admins can:

  • Search, view, and manage all tenant workspaces
  • Override entitlements and storage quotas per tenant
  • Suspend and restore tenant accounts
  • Assign storage tiers (LTD, Basic, Essentials, Premium, Enterprise)
  • Manage platform-level credentials for Google, Stripe, Twilio, OpenAI, Bunny, and Reoon
  • Impersonate tenants for support (all impersonation actions are audit-logged)
  • Monitor system health, notifications, and workflow activity
