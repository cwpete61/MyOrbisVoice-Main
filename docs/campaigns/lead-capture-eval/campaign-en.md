## Cross-platform notes
- **One destination:** every asset points to the Lead Capture Score quiz. Don't split traffic.
- **Lead with the leak, close with the number.** PAS for the body, the score + dollar figure as the payoff.
- **Scarcity must be real.** Pick an actual monthly beta cap and hold it. Drop "limited spots" the month you're not capped.
- **No orphan stats.** If you add an industry statistic to any caption, attach a real source per the style guide.
- **Verticalize when you can:** swap the generic scene for the audience — dental ("the patient with jaw pain at 6pm"), legal ("the injured caller who hires whoever answers"), home services ("the burst pipe at 9pm"), fitness ("the lead who DMs at 11pm"), beauty ("she books with whoever answers first").
