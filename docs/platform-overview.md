**AI-Powered Voice Automation for Modern Businesses**

## Turn Every Call Into a Business Opportunity — Automatically

MyOrbisVoice is a full-stack AI voice automation platform that answers your phones, handles your website visitors, and runs your outbound calling campaigns — around the clock, without hiring additional staff. Every interaction is logged, transcribed, summarized, and available instantly from your dashboard.

It is not a call center. It is not a chatbot. It is a trained, real-time voice AI that speaks, listens, books appointments, answers questions, qualifies leads, and escalates to a human when necessary — all managed from a single browser-based dashboard.

---

## Three Communication Channels

**📞 Inbound Receptionist**
Answers every inbound call, greets callers by your brand name, handles questions, books appointments, and escalates to a live person when needed. Fully operational after hours — callers are never sent to a dead line.

**🌐 Website Voice Widget**
Embedded directly on your website. Visitors click a button to speak with your AI agent in real time — no phone number required. Works on desktop and mobile browsers.

**📤 Outbound Caller**
Runs automated outbound calling campaigns to your contact list. Follow-ups, appointment reminders, lead re-engagement, and qualification calls — executed at scale with no manual dialing.

## Core Features

**Business DNA — Your Agent Knows Your Business**
Every account is configured with a Business DNA profile — a structured knowledge base covering your business identity, services, pricing, operating hours, lead qualification rules, appointment logic, escalation conditions, language preferences, compliance rules, and prohibited topics. The AI agent draws from this in every conversation automatically.

**Real-Time AI Voice Conversations**
Powered by Google Gemini Live for real-time voice and OpenAI GPT-4o for reasoning. The agent listens, responds, handles interruptions naturally, detects silence, and knows when to transfer, book, or escalate. Conversations feel natural — not robotic.

**Appointment Booking — Fully Automated**
The agent books directly into Google Calendar during the call. It checks live availability, offers alternative slots if needed, confirms the booking verbally, and sends a confirmation email. Zero manual follow-up required.

**Word-for-Word Call Transcripts**
Every conversation is transcribed line by line, labeled "Caller" and "Agent," and stored in your dashboard. No audio review needed — just read exactly what was said.

**AI Call Summaries**
Every call also receives a 2–3 sentence AI-written summary so you can scan what happened at a glance without reading the full transcript.

**Voice Recordings**
All calls are recorded and stored securely in cloud storage. Accessible from your conversation history with a click-to-play link. Storage quotas and retention periods are configurable per account.

**Contact Management**
Full contact database with complete call history, SMS history, opt-out status per channel (voice, SMS, email), email validation, and a full activity timeline per contact. Inbound callers are automatically matched to existing contacts.

**Two-Way SMS Messaging**
Send and receive SMS through the platform. STOP/START keywords are automatically detected and honored. Opt-out status is enforced before any outbound SMS is sent. All messages are logged to the contact timeline.

**Outbound Campaigns**
Build and launch outbound calling campaigns to your contact list. Set the script, target audience, retry policy, and schedule. Outcomes, recordings, and results are tracked per call.

## AI Agent Roles

Each business configures one or more agent roles — each with its own personality, prompt, and allowed actions:

- **Orchestrator** — routes and coordinates between other agents
- **Appointment Agent** — booking, rescheduling, and calendar management
- **Sales Agent** — lead qualification, product presentation, objection handling
- **Customer Service Agent** — issue resolution, account questions, support escalation
- **Marketing Agent** — campaign outreach, promotions, survey collection
- **Assistant** — general-purpose help for any caller need
- **Secretary** — message taking, transferring, and scheduling

## Integrations

**Google (Gmail & Calendar)**
Connect a dedicated Google account via OAuth. The AI agent sends booking confirmations, follow-up emails, and manages calendar events directly — no manual work required. Credentials are stored encrypted and never exposed.

**Twilio**
All phone calls and SMS messages run through Twilio. Inbound routing, outbound dialing, call recording, voicemail, SMS delivery, and forwarding are all managed through the platform.

**Stripe**
Subscription billing, plan upgrades, and invoice management are handled through Stripe. Tenants manage their own billing from the dashboard.

**OpenAI & Google Gemini**
OpenAI GPT-4o handles reasoning, summarization, and orchestration. Google Gemini Live handles the real-time spoken conversation. Both are available to every tenant automatically — no setup required per account.

## Security & Compliance

- All credentials stored AES-256 encrypted — never visible in the UI or API responses
- Complete tenant isolation — no cross-account data access is possible
- Role-based access control — Owner, Manager, Staff, and Affiliate roles per workspace
- Immutable audit logs for all sensitive actions (billing changes, secret rotation, impersonation)
- SMS opt-out compliance — STOP/START enforced automatically per contact, per channel
- Signed recording URLs — audio files are never publicly accessible
- Storage quota monitoring with automatic warnings and recording pause at limit
- Webhook signature validation on all Twilio and Stripe events

---

## Platform Administration

A full admin control surface is available to platform operators, separate from the tenant-facing dashboard. Admins can:

- Search, view, and manage all tenant workspaces
- Override entitlements and storage quotas per tenant
- Suspend and restore tenant accounts
- Assign storage tiers (LTD, Basic, Essentials, Premium, Enterprise)
- Manage platform-level credentials for Google, Stripe, Twilio, OpenAI, Bunny, and Reoon
- Impersonate tenants for support (all impersonation actions are audit-logged)
- Monitor system health, notifications, and workflow activity
