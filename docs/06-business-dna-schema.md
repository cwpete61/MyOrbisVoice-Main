# Business DNA schema

Business DNA is the tenant-specific knowledge and behavior layer that personalizes all agents.

## Required sections

### Identity
- business summary
- years in operation
- service region
- core value statement

### Services
- services offered
- services not offered
- primary offer categories
- upsell and cross-sell opportunities

### Pricing
- pricing language
- quote policy
- estimate rules
- discount rules
- financing notes if applicable

### Operations
- operating hours
- holiday behavior
- response-time promise
- staffing model
- after-hours rules

### Sales behavior
- lead qualification criteria
- ideal customer profile
- objection handling rules
- close strategy rules
- disallowed pressure tactics

### Appointment behavior
- appointment types
- duration defaults
- booking windows
- cancellation rules
- reschedule rules
- alternative-slot rules

### Service behavior
- common support issues
- escalation triggers
- human handoff conditions
- emergency handling notes

### Language control
- preferred words
- words to avoid
- brand tone
- local phrases if applicable
- mandatory disclosures

### Compliance
- prohibited claims
- restricted topics
- regulated service notes
- privacy handling notes

## Output use

Business DNA is injected into:
- role prompts
- channel prompts
- follow-up messaging
- summaries and handoff notes
