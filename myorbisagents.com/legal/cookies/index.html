Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how MyOrbisAgents (a product of MyOrbisResults) uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and dashboards.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help a website function, remember your preferences, and understand how it is used.

2. Cookies we use

  • Strictly necessary — required to sign in, keep you authenticated, and secure the Service. These cannot be turned off.
  • Preferences — remember settings such as language.
  • Analytics — help us understand aggregate usage so we can improve the Service.

The Orby voice widget stores a short-lived session identifier so a conversation can continue; it is not used for advertising.

3. Third-party cookies

Some cookies are set by providers we use, such as authentication, payment (Stripe), and analytics services. Their use is governed by their own policies.

4. Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking strictly necessary cookies may prevent parts of the Service from working.

5. Changes

We may update this Policy and will post the new version here with an updated date.
